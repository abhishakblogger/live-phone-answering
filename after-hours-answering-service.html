An after hours answering service is a professional B2B solution where live receptionists handle your incoming calls outside of standard business hours. An after hours answering service covers calls received outside of standard business hours, typically from 5 PM to 9 AM on weekdays, plus all day on weekends and public holidays. Yes, a premium after hours answering service can easily handle emergency calls. We utilize custom urgency protocols to filter standard inquiries from true emergencies.
