How much does a live phone answering service cost?

A live phone answering service typically costs between $39 to $250 per month, depending on your call volume and required features. We offer transparent, pay-per-call and monthly minute plans with no hidden fees and no long-term contracts.

What is the difference between a live answering service and a call center?

A live answering service acts as a seamless extension of your local business, focusing on personalized reception, lead capture, and appointment scheduling. A traditional call center handles massive, impersonal inbound volume, lacking the tailored, localized touch your high-ticket clients expect.

Can a live answering service work for my small business?

Yes, a live answering service is ideal for small businesses and local contractors. It ensures you never miss a lead while out on a job site, instantly elevating your brand's professionalism and allowing you to compete with much larger companies.

How quickly are calls answered by your receptionists?

Our standard is to answer every incoming call in under four rings. We prioritize lightning-fast response times to ensure your callers immediately speak with a live human, eliminating wait times and preventing high-value leads from abandoning the call.

Is there a free trial for live phone answering?

Yes, we offer a risk-free, 7-day free trial so you can experience the impact of our live phone answering service firsthand. Setup takes less than five minutes, and you can test our call handling and lead capture before committing to a plan.

Do your receptionists answer calls in Spanish?

Yes, we provide fully bilingual live phone answering services. Our fluent English and Spanish-speaking receptionists ensure you never turn away a potential customer due to a language barrier, allowing you to expand your reach and capture more leads in diverse markets.

What happens to my calls after business hours?

We provide 24/7/365 coverage. After your normal business hours, our live receptionists continue to answer calls using your customized script. We can capture standard messages for the next day, or instantly dispatch emergency requests to your on-call team via text or app notifications.
