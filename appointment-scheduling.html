Frequently Asked Questions About Appointment Scheduling

How does the live scheduling work?

Our receptionists access your scheduling software (Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity, or your EMR) and book appointments directly based on your availability and rules. You receive an instant notification with the patient's or client's details.

Can you handle HIPAA-compliant medical scheduling?

Yes, we are fully HIPAA certified. We sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) and our receptionists are specifically trained in PHI handling and medical triage to ensure your practice remains compliant.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Our team can handle cancellations and rescheduling just like your in-house staff. We follow your specific policies regarding notice periods and rescheduling fees.
